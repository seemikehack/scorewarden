About ScoreWarden

ScoreWarden is a simple scorekeeper for all sorts of games.

It can track up to eight players. You can also use the following keyboard shortcuts to update scores for each of the eight players:

1-8 Select player one through eight
Q/A Increment/decrement score by 1
W/S Increment/decrement score by 5
E/D Increment/decrement score by 20
